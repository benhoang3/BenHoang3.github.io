About Me

My name is Ben Hoang, and I am from Bothell, Washington, where I have spent most of my life and continue to reside. As a proud Vietnamese-American and second-generation college student, I carry the legacy of my family’s resilience while forging my own path. Currently, I am pursuing a bachelor's degree in Business Administration with a concentration in Management Information Systems, a field that aligns with my interest in solving complex problems and creating efficient systems. My short-term goal is to land an internship that will allow me to apply what I’ve learned in the classroom to real-world situations. Long-term, I aspire to build a fulfilling career leveraging my degree to drive innovation and success. A challenge that has shaped me is striving to live up to the success of my parents. Their accomplishments inspired me but also created a sense of pressure. Over time, I developed the perspective that my journey is unique and equally valuable, which helped me overcome this struggle. This experience instilled in me perseverance and confidence, qualities I value deeply and apply to both academics and life. I value these qualities deeply and apply them to both my academics and my life. I am committed to working hard, continually learning, and contributing meaningfully to my field. With determination and passion for making a positive impact, I am eager to create a lasting difference wherever my career path leads.
